FAQ

Common questions.

Is your meat really halal certified?

Yes. All products come from USDA-approved, halal-certified suppliers. We can share certification on request.

What is the minimum order?

Minimum order is $35. Delivery is $8.99 flat. Orders over $75 receive free delivery.

When do I need to order for next-day delivery?

Order by 5:00 PM and you will receive delivery the following day, Tuesday through Sunday.

Do you deliver to my area?

We currently deliver across Katy, Cinco Ranch, West Houston, Sugar Land, and Richmond. WhatsApp us your ZIP to confirm.

Can I place a large order for Eid?

Absolutely. For large orders (10 lbs+), please WhatsApp us at least 48 hours in advance.

How do I pay?

Secure online payment via Stripe. You'll be redirected to Stripe's checkout after placing your order.
